our mission

To Help Free Folks from crippling student debt by bringing together those who have much, with those who have not, and inspire them both unto greatness.

At PYR, we believe that everyone is capable of accomplishing great things if they are given the opportunity, and encouraged to pursue their dreams.

PYR is dedicated to helping people overcome a part of one of the worst obstacles to their future progress and financial growth- their student debt.

By giving each person a platform to share their story of how they are working towards a better life and moving the world forward, we believe that they will inspire others to contribute to their pursuits and support their progress.

Everything we do at PYR is focused on encouraging people to explore their passion and inspire others to pursue their own greatness.

Here at PYR, we’re working to Inspire Fire.
