the 60-second scholarships

What could you do with an extra $100?

Gas. Groceries. Rent. Let's face it, living ain't cheap. We know that you're working hard to keep on the lights, but sometimes a little extra help can go a long way. That's why we here at PYR (pronounced pyre) created "The 60-Second Scholarship", a totally new type of scholarship designed to put more money in the pockets of those who could use it most: everyone.

How Does It Work?

It's very simply

  1. Sign Up
  2. Create a 30-60 second video explaining
    1. Who you are
    2. What you're doing to better yourself or someone else
    3. Why you need $100 OR What you would use $100 for
  3. Upload your video to Youtube
  4. Add the video link to your application
  5. Submit your application
    6. Wait
  6. If your application is chosen, you will receive an email with a link to your "Campaign Video"
  7. This Campaign Video features your video along with the videos of three other candidates
  8. Share this Campaign Video with as many of your friends, family members, enemies, and acquaintances as you'd like, and tell them to vote for you! (Voting takes place directly on the video)
  9. At the end of two weeks (14 consecutive days), the candidate with the most votes wins!
    1. The Winner will be alerted via email and receive a check for $100 in the mail within 3 weeks.

What's the Catch?

As we all know, there is no such thing as a free lunch. Simply put, people want to know what we get out of this. The answer: eyes.

Each applicant, video, and vote creates both content and community. The plan is to grow the PYR family- a community of students, graduates, creators, and professionals- to a critical mass where we can start generating additional revenue from advertisements.

We then take a portion of that money to cover administrative costs, and then we will put the vast majority of the rest right back into growing the PYR community by offering more scholarships worth more money to more people.
